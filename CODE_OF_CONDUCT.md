# Code of Conduct

TeamCast is an open source project and aims to keep collaboration respectful, direct, and technically useful.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful in disagreements.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Keep feedback specific and actionable.
- Assume good intent, but stay rigorous about technical claims.
- Respect other contributors' time, boundaries, and privacy.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, intimidation, or bad-faith disruption.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeatedly derailing discussions or spamming maintainers.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other content that violates this policy. Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent bans from the project spaces.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer privately using the contact details available on the GitHub profile rather than escalating a public thread.
